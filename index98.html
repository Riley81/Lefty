Lefty's

Being a "lefty", is unique by nature, only 10% of the population is left-handed. Having a left hand for a dominant hand certainly has its perks. Perks such as reportedly recovering from strokes quicker, as left-handed people are believed to strengthen both sides of the brain; perks like having an advantage in many sports, due to players being accustomed to right handed opponents. Left-handed people can even supposedly solve complex problems more efficiently, as they can use both sides of the brain more readily. Most importantly I think, it makes them, unique
